How to Get Free .me Domain [Students Only]

11 March 2017 | Reading time: 1 min

Recent posts

  • » How to Get Free .me Domain [Students Only]

NameCheap in partnership with .ME TLD and GitHub provides a free .me domain for all students. You can use your GitHub repository as a free hosting with it. All you need for this is university email confirmed GitHub account. So let's get started.

Step 1:

Head over to nc.me and enter you desired domain name into search form. Next, click Search button.



Step 2:

Click ADD button to add your choosen domain name into "basket", and click Complete Order button.



Step 3:

Scroll down to "Student email" section. Enter your school email. Press Finish Up button. They will send you the conformational mail, make sure to confirm it.



Step 4:

Head over to NameCheap.com and login into your account if you haven't already.



Step 5:

When you're logged in you'll see your dashboard. In Recently Active in Your Account section you'll see your domain name. Click on the manage button on the right.



Step 6:

In the newly opened page make sure that Namecheap BasicDNS is selected under nameservers section. Now click on the Advance DNS tab.



Final step:

Under host records section click Add new record button. Add following IPs as shown bellow (make sure that you change username to yours under third record):


->Host: @ | Value: 192.30.252.153

->Host: @ | Value: 192.30.252.154

->Host: www| Value: username.github.io



It will take about 30 minutes to update nameserver records so be patient. That's it enjoy!
